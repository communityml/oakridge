Our Mission

The Heart of Oakridge

Grounded in Values. United in Community. Committed to Excellence.

Oakridge begins a renewed chapter rooted in what has always defined us: faith, academic rigor, character, and love. Our community comes together with conviction to preserve something extraordinary, a place where children are known, challenged, and deeply supported.

We hold high expectations in the classroom while fostering respect, compassion, and a genuine sense of home among our students and families. A rigorous education and a genuinely warm community don't just coexist at Oakridge. They strengthen each other.

Character guides us.

A Fresh Start

It has been just a few days since Oakridge was saved from an acquisition that threatened to erase Oakridge's unique character and offering. Here's the roadmap ahead.

The Roadmap

1
Community Mobilization

Bring parents, alumni, and supporters together to give Oakridge the extra support it needs.

2
Governance, Board Improvements, and Strategic Plan

Build the organizational foundation Oakridge needs: a strengthened board, clear governance, and a multi-year strategic plan developed in partnership with the community.

3
Fundraising

Grow fundraising efforts by engaging families, alumni, and community partners to build the financial support this school deserves.

4
Building an Endowment

Create lasting financial stability so Oakridge is never again one difficult year away from crisis. An endowment built for the next generation.

5
Growing Oakridge

Expand enrollment, strengthen programs, and position Oakridge to thrive for decades to come.

What Has Been Accomplished

  • Bringing the community together to save Oakridge
  • Establishing a direct connection between the board and the community
  • Getting transparency into the solvable challenges that Oakridge has been trying to face on its own so that we can get the right help in place

What's Next

The Plan Will Be Built Together

We need people willing to bring their expertise, their networks, and their time to the table. The areas where help is most needed:

Governance & board structure

Helping Oakridge build the organizational foundation to carry the school forward sustainably, with structures that serve the school's mission for the long term.

Strategy & planning

Enrollment growth, program development, long-term positioning. Turning the community's energy into a real, durable multi-year plan.

Fundraising & development

Building the endowment and a systematic fundraising function so the school is never one hard year away from crisis again.

Finance, law & marketing

Professional expertise that small independent schools rarely have access to. If you have it, Oakridge needs you.

Oakridge school culture is transparent, principled, and forward-thinking, preparing students not only for academic achievement, but for lives of integrity, leadership, and service.

Founded 45 years ago on the belief that every child has strengths worth nurturing, Oakridge was built on character, faith, and the conviction that a school should be open to everyone who shares those values. It's the kind of school families come back to. Alumni send their own children here.

The Six Pillars aren't a poster on a wall. They leave a mark that lasts a lifetime.

Like many independent schools, Oakridge reached a critical crossroads. Enrollment pressure and the absence of a strong endowment made long-term stability uncertain. Tuition alone has limits. The community came together at exactly the right moment.

Now the work continues. Families and alumni are working alongside school leadership to build the structure this school deserves: a strategy, an endowment, and a governance foundation that can carry Oakridge into its next chapter.
