Ways you can help (pick one)

1) Donate

If you’re able, a donation helps stabilize the school and fund the essentials. We’ll point you to the best way to give and make sure your support is recognized.

2) Volunteer

Help with outreach, events, communications, alumni connections, fundraising coordination, and more. Even one hour helps.

3) Connect us

Know an alum, grandparent, local business, or community leader who loves Oakridge? Introductions are powerful.

4) Share

Share this page with Oakridge families and alumni. The fastest way to help is to widen the circle.

Why this exists

Oakridge is more than a school

For decades, Oakridge has been a place where children grow in confidence, character, and community. It’s the kind of school families come back to — sometimes even enrolling the next generation.

This effort is about bringing people together — parents, alumni, grandparents, friends of the school, and the amazing teachers and staff — so Oakridge can move forward with strength.

We’ll keep this page updated as our community plans take shape (and we’ll add photos soon).
