Why this exists

Oakridge is more than a school

The school needs the community’s energy, ideas, and support — to survive and to build the foundation for an enduring legacy. Oakridge families are working with school leadership to organize a simple on-ramp for people who want to help.

For decades, Oakridge has been a place where children grow in confidence, character, and community. It’s the kind of school families come back to — sometimes even enrolling the next generation.

This effort is about all of us — parents, alumni, grandparents, friends of the school, and the amazing teachers and staff — moving forward together.
