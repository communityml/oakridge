Our Mission

The Heart of Oakridge

Grounded in Values. United in Community. Committed to Excellence.

Oakridge begins a renewed chapter rooted in what has always defined us: faith, academic rigor, character, and love. Our community comes together with conviction to preserve something extraordinary — a place where children are known, challenged, and deeply supported.

We hold high expectations in the classroom while fostering respect, compassion, and a genuine sense of home among our students and families. A rigorous education and a genuinely warm community don't just coexist at Oakridge — they strengthen each other.

Character guides us.

Community holds us together.

Every child is known here.

We are committed to honoring the trust of our families through a school culture that is transparent, principled, and forward-thinking — preparing students not only for academic achievement, but for lives of integrity, leadership, and service.

To our families — you are the heart of Oakridge.
To our supporters — you are part of something lasting.
To those discovering us — welcome. You will feel the difference.

Founded 45 years ago on the belief that every child has strengths worth nurturing, Oakridge was built on character, faith, and the conviction that a school should be open to everyone who shares those values. It's the kind of school families come back to. Alumni send their own children here. Teachers stay for decades. The Six Pillars aren't a poster on a wall — they leave a mark that lasts a lifetime.

Like many independent schools, Oakridge reached a critical crossroads — enrollment pressure and the absence of a strong endowment made long-term stability uncertain. Tuition alone has limits. The community came together at exactly the right moment.

Now the work continues. Families and alumni are working alongside school leadership to build the structure this school deserves — a strategy, an endowment, and a governance foundation that can carry Oakridge into its next chapter.

What's Next

The plan will be built together

There is no single leader and no finished roadmap — and that's intentional. What comes next for Oakridge will be shaped by this community, in close collaboration with school leadership. The people who love this school most are the ones best positioned to help it thrive.

We need people willing to bring their expertise, their networks, and their time to the table. The areas where help is most needed:

Governance & board structure

Helping Oakridge build the organizational foundation to carry the school forward sustainably — structures that serve the school's mission for the long term.

Strategy & planning

Enrollment growth, program development, long-term positioning. Turning the community's energy into a real, durable multi-year plan.

Fundraising & development

Building the endowment and a systematic fundraising function — so the school is never one hard year away from crisis again.

Finance, law & marketing

Professional expertise that small independent schools rarely have access to. If you have it, Oakridge needs you.
