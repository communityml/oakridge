Oakridge Is Not Going Anywhere.

For over 45 years, Oakridge Private School has been more than a school — it's been a family. A place where children learn to lead with character, think with curiosity, and grow with love.

We've faced tough times. But our community has always been our greatest strength. Right now, we need that strength more than ever — and together, we're writing a bold new chapter.

❤️ Love 🌟 Character 🌍 Diversity 💡 Innovation 🤝 Community 📚 High Standards

These are the six pillars that have always defined Oakridge. They always will.

Our Story

45 Years of Excellence

Oakridge was founded by Mr. and Mrs. Burry, who recognized a growing educational need in Orange County. After spending two decades teaching in the public school system, they built a school rooted in small class sizes, strong academics, and genuine care for every child.

"The greatest testament to Oakridge's success is the many former students who have chosen to enroll their own children."

What began with just 9 students has grown into a beloved institution — one where alumni come back as parents because they know what Oakridge means.
