Why this exists

Oakridge is more than a school

Oakridge has meant so much to so many families. Right now, the school needs the community’s energy, ideas, and support — and we’re organizing a simple on-ramp for people who want to help.

For decades, Oakridge has been a place where children grow in confidence, character, and community. It’s the kind of school families come back to — sometimes even enrolling the next generation.

This effort is about bringing people together — parents, alumni, grandparents, friends of the school, and the amazing teachers and staff — so Oakridge can move forward with strength.
