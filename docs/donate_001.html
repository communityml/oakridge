Oakridge has served this community for 45 years. Every donation — large or small — helps ensure it's here for the next 45.

Why Your Gift Matters

Oakridge was recently saved from an acquisition that would have permanently altered — or ended — the school as this community knows it. That was a close call. Your donation is part of making sure it never happens again.

The work ahead is real: strengthening governance, growing the endowment, building a fundraising foundation the school has never had. None of that happens without community support.
