Ways to Give

Invest in the Future of Oakridge

Oakridge is entering a bold new chapter — one rooted in faith, strengthened by community, and committed to academic excellence for every child. Your generosity directly fuels this mission.

Every gift supports innovative programs, academics, character development, and a diverse, welcoming environment where students are known and loved. As we step into an exciting new era of growth and excellence, your support makes it possible. Give today and join us in building the future.

To discuss a donation, planned giving, or a partnership, contact our donations team directly. donations@oakridgeschool.org

Donate

Every dollar goes directly to Oakridge Private School — a 501(c)(3) nonprofit — and helps build the lasting foundation this school deserves. Click below to make a secure online donation through Stripe, one of the world's most trusted payment platforms. It takes less than two minutes and you can give any amount you choose.

Your gift strengthens Oakridge's endowment, supports exceptional teachers, and helps ensure this school thrives for the next generation of families.

In-Kind Donation

Support Oakridge with goods, services, or materials. Every in-kind contribution — large or small — directly benefits our students and programs.

To submit an in-kind donation, please provide:

  • Name
  • Organization / Company Name (if applicable)
  • Email
  • Donation Details
  • Delivery Method (Drop-off, Pick-up, or Shipped)
  • Additional Info
